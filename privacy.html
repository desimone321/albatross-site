Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. If you upload a profile picture, that image is stored securely. We also collect data about your pool activity, picks, and scores to provide the core app experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate the app, manage your pools and picks, calculate leaderboard scores, and send you notifications about deadlines and results. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Albatross uses Firebase (Google) for authentication, data storage, and push notifications. We use DataGolf, RapidAPI, and The Odds API to provide player statistics, tournament data, and betting odds. These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore with security rules that ensure users can only access their own data and the pools they belong to. Profile pictures are stored in Firebase Storage with authenticated access controls.

5. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for pick deadlines, score updates, and pool activity. You can manage notification preferences in the app settings or through your device's notification settings.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Albatross is not intended for users under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app displays betting odds for informational purposes, which is why it carries a 17+ age rating.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or by email.

9. Contact Us

Privacy questions? Contact us at Harrisondesimone@live.com
