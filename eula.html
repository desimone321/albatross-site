3. Description of Service

Albatross is a social fantasy golf application that enables users to create and participate in private golf pools with friends. Users form groups, select golfers for upcoming PGA tournaments, and compete based on real-world player performance. The Application provides three game modes: Tiered Pick, Salary Cap, and One and Done.

Albatross is designed for private friend groups — it is not a daily fantasy sports (DFS) platform, sportsbook, or wagering service. No real money is wagered between pool members through the Application. Betting odds displayed in the Application's Research tab are sourced from third-party providers and are for informational and analytical purposes only; users cannot place bets through Albatross.

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID account. Payment is charged at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
